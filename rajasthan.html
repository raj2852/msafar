ABOUT RAJASTHAN

KNOWN AS THE PLACE OF THE RAJPUTS,RAJASHAN ALSO HAS MANY BEAUTIFUL ARCHITECTURES OF THE PAST.THE CULTURE, MUSIC, FOLK STORIES ALL FORM AN IMPORTANT PART OF THIS STATE.ONCE FAMOUS FOR THE GREAT WARRIORS ,RAJASTHAN EVEN NOW HAS THEIR HEIRS DWELLING.COME AND ENJOY THE DESERTS AND THE ARAVALI HILLS.
